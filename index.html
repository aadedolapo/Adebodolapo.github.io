CAR PRICE PREDICTION

In this project, a dataset of anonymized vehicle adverts was processed for machine learning. Missing values in the Year_of_registration and Reg_Code columns were filled using web scraping and mapping. Outliers in Mileage and Price were capped and handled accordingly. Categorical encoding was applied to various columns, and feature engineering techniques, including creating new columns and using polynomial features, were implemented.
Feature selection was performed using Recursive Feature Elimination with Cross Validation(RFECV) The Catboost model achieved the best overall performance, with a training score of 97.5%, RMSE of ¬£2344.412, and R-squared of 93.0%.
A true vs. predicted analysis revealed that the model performed well for lower-priced cars but had larger residuals for higher-priced ones. SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) was used for global and local explanations of the model's predictions.

YORUBA PROVERB GENERATOR USING NEURAL NETWORK

I lead a team of 4 Data Scientist in building an NLG project. The Yoruba proverbs generator app uses a model that takes in a keyword or phrase in Yoruba language and generates a proverb in Yoruba language from the given keyword or phrase.
